Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to restore my Lifetime VIP?
A: Open Settings in Calclite and tap "Restore Purchase". Ensure you're signed in with the Apple ID used for purchase.
Q: How can I delete my account?
A: Go to Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove your email and data from our servers.

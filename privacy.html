Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2026

CalcLite (hereinafter referred to as "we") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your personal information.

1. How We Collect and Use Information

Camera Permission & OCR

Purpose: Used for the "OCR Smart Recognition" feature.

Explanation: Images are uploaded to a cloud OCR interface for instant recognition only. We do NOT permanently save your private receipt images on our servers. After recognition, images are saved only in your phone's local database.

Account Information

Purpose: User login and membership binding.

Explanation: We collect your Email Address as a unique identifier. This is used solely to sync your VIP subscription status, NOT to sync your ledger data.

Ledger Data (Special Notice)

All income, expenses, and notes are stored entirely in your device's local database. We cannot view, export, analyze, or backup your personal financial status.

2. Information Storage

Location: Business data generated by the software is stored locally on your device.

Duration: Local data is retained as long as you do not delete or uninstall the app. Once uninstalled, data cannot be recovered.

3. Third-Party SDKs

  • UniApp SDK: Provides basic application framework services.
  • Apple IAP: Handles your membership subscription and payment requests.

4. Your Rights & Account Deletion

Account Cancellation: You can request to delete your account via Settings > Delete Account. Upon deletion, your cloud account info (Email and VIP status) will be removed.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

1. Information Collection and Use

Camera Permission and Image Information

When you use the "OCR Smart Recognition" feature, we require access to your Camera or Photo Album. Images are uploaded to a cloud OCR interface for instant recognition only. We do NOT permanently store your private receipt images on our servers. After recognition, data is saved only in your device's local database.

Account Information

We collect your Email Address as a unique identifier. This is used solely for user authentication and to sync your VIP subscription status. We do NOT sync or store your ledger data on our servers.

Ledger Data

All financial entries, including income, expenses, and notes, are stored entirely on your device's local database. We cannot view, export, analyze, or back up your personal financial information.

2. Data Storage and Security

Business data generated by the software is stored locally on your mobile terminal. Local data is retained as long as you do not delete or uninstall the app. Once uninstalled, the data cannot be recovered by us.

3. Account Deletion

In compliance with Apple's guidelines, users can initiate Account Deletion within the app via Settings > Delete Account. Upon deletion, your cloud-based account identifiers (email and VIP status) will be permanently removed from our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple IAP: Used to handle your membership subscription and payment requests.
  • UniApp SDK: Provides basic application framework services.
